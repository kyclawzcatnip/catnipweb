May 05, 2026 Dev Blog

Project Anti-Catite Physics System Overhaul

In Dev Log #14, our physics engine lead breaks down the math behind the gravity vector shifts. We have transitioned from standard Newtonian gravity constants to a localized vector fields grid, fixing the collision jitters that occurred when players flipped gravity while running along platforms.
